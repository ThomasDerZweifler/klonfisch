Top committers today:

SELECT COUNT(*) as commits, c_author
FROM `commits`
WHERE DATE(c_date) = CURDATE()
GROUP BY c_author
ORDER BY commits DESC

Top committers week:

SELECT COUNT(*) as commits, c_author
FROM commits
WHERE YEAR(c_date) = YEAR(CURDATE()) AND WEEK(c_date) = WEEK(CURDATE())
GROUP BY c_author
ORDER BY commits DESC
